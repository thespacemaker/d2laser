How do you play at D2 Fortress?

D2 Tactical Laser Tag Battle Box Drive-Thru!

All participants are given state of the art ASHQ Laser Tag equipment with a 30mm Red Dot Scope, then given instructions and placed in a massive 16,000 sq ft battlefield affectionately named "The Fortress" by our players.

Players then engage in unique one of a kind fast-paced, referee facilitated, adrenaline-packed missions like Capture the Flag, Team Domination, King of the Hill, Zombie Infection, Defender and many others.
